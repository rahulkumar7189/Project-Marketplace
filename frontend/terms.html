Terms & Conditions

1. Academic Integrity

AcadMate is a peer-to-peer academic support platform. Users agree to use the platform solely for guidance, clarification of concepts, and reference solutions. Users must not use AcadMate for direct assignment completion, exam cheating, or any form of academic misconduct.

2. Advance Payment Policy

To ensure commitment and security for both students and helpers, AcadMate implements a mandatory advance payment system:

  • Amount: A 50% advance payment of the agreed budget is required.
  • Activation: Helper contact details (Phone Number) are only unlocked and revealed to the student once the advance payment is confirmed.
  • Security: This payment acts as a guarantee for the helper's time and resources.

3. User Conduct

Professional behavior is expected at all times. Harassment, spam, or sharing of inappropriate content will lead to immediate account suspension and a permanent ban from the platform.

4. Refund Policy

Advance payments are typically non-refundable unless the helper fails to provide the requested guidance within the specified deadline. Disputes can be raised through the Admin Dashboard for manual review.
